What is the "Clicker"?

✔ Clicker is the Audience Response System that enables instructors and students to interact dynamically through question-and-polling.

✔ Clicker is helpful when the instructor wants to evaluate how many students understand the key concepts of the lecture correctly.

✔ More than a half-million students use clicker on several thousand college campuses including Harvard, the University of Arizona and Vanderbilt. (Nov 15, 2010, The New York Times)

✔ Eric Mazur, the professor of Harvard physics department and the pioneer of Peer Instruction, introduced clicker as the essential tool in his instruction.
